ElastiCache

Elasticache is in-memory database. It helps to relieve the load in RDS.

Elasticache Types

  • Memcached
    • Non persistent
    • No backup and restore
    • No high availability
  • Redis
    • Multi AZ with auto-failover
    • Backup and restore features
    • Read replicas and high availability
